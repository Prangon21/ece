About Me

I am a Graduate Research Assistant in Electrical & Electronic Engineering at Ahsanullah University of Science and Technology (AUST), Dhaka, Bangladesh. My work spans energy systems optimization, renewable energy integration, wireless power transfer, and power electronics.

I hold concurrent positions as a Graduate Research Assistant at both AUST and Brac University. I also serve as an active peer reviewer for journals published by Elsevier, Springer Nature, Nature Portfolio, Sage, and Tech Science Press.

My M.Sc. thesis focuses on the design of a co-optimized wireless power transfer system for vehicle-to-vehicle (V2V) applications, supervised by Prof. Omar Farrok.
